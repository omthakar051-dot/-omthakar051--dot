How evoyt works

From scattered data to a compliant passport

Four steps. Your suppliers upload once — evoyt handles the standards, verification and hosting.

01

Capture

Suppliers add certificates, composition and test data via a dead-simple mobile/WhatsApp-grade flow — in their language.

02

Verify

evoyt structures and validates data into the EU schema — secured with blockchain-backed, tamper-proof verifiable credentials.

03

Generate

A unique ID + QR/Data Matrix passport is created to EN 18219/18220 and GS1 standards.

04

Share

Buyers, regulators and consumers scan to see the right view — hosted in the EU, for the product's life.
